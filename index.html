AMICUS

AI-powered email briefing assistant by AzulLogic

AMICUS connects to your Google or Microsoft email account and delivers a clear, prioritised daily brief every morning — so you always know what needs your attention without spending an hour in your inbox.

What AMICUS does

  • Summarises your inbox into a prioritised daily email brief
  • Learns which topics and senders matter most to you
  • Delivers your brief at a time and timezone you choose
  • Provides inbox analytics — volume trends, busiest senders, response times
  • Sends push and calendar reminders to help you plan your day

How it works

  1. Connect your Google or Microsoft email account
  2. Set your priorities — topics and senders that matter to you
  3. Receive a concise daily brief every morning
