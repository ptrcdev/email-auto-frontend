AMICUS

AI Email Assistant

AMICUS is an AI-powered email assistant that helps users organise their inbox by automatically summarising emails, identifying important messages, and providing a daily overview of incoming communications.

Sign in to AMICUS

What AMICUS does

  • Connects securely to your Gmail or Microsoft Outlook account with your explicit permission
  • Reads your emails to generate AI-powered summaries and insights
  • Highlights your most important incoming messages each day
  • Delivers a concise daily brief of your inbox every morning
  • Helps you stay on top of your inbox without spending hours reading emails

Why AMICUS requests Google access

AMICUS requests access to your Gmail account only to retrieve emails that you explicitly authorise. Your data is processed solely to generate summaries and improve your email management experience. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

AMICUS's use of data received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Privacy & Security

AMICUS is operated by AzulLogic. We never sell your personal data or email content to third parties. AMICUS complies with GDPR and applicable data protection law.
